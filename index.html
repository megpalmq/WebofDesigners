About this project

A curated study of designers whose ideas shaped modern design — built for Helen and anyone who wants practical inspiration from history.

What this site is

A searchable, digestible collection of designers, their philosophies, and key works. Each profile links ideas to practice so you can see how big concepts translate into real design decisions.

Why it matters

Design is a conversation across time. Knowing where ideas come from gives context to modern trends and teaches you how to borrow, adapt, and innovate responsibly.

Who it's for

Students, designers, educators, and curious minds — anyone wanting a quick, meaningful way to connect historical influence to contemporary practice.

Start by exploring a designer — or try the design study cards to discover unexpected influences.
