Web of Designers.

Tracing the expressive threads of design history, from the modernist grid to today's digital culture.

About this project

A curated study and digital archive dedicated to the pioneers of modern design. This site connects their fundamental philosophies to contemporary design challenges, serving as a practical reference for students and professionals.

What this site is

A searchable, digestible collection of designers, their core philosophies, and key works. Each profile links historical ideas to modern practice, illustrating how big concepts translate into current design decisions.

Why it matters

Design is a continuous conversation across time. Understanding the origins of concepts gives context to modern trends and teaches us how to borrow, adapt, and innovate with responsibility and depth.

Who it's for

Students, designers, educators, and curious minds — anyone wanting a quick, meaningful way to connect historical influence to contemporary practice.
