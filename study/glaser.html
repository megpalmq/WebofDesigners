About Milton Glaser

Milton Glaser (1929–2020) was a visionary graphic designer whose work defined American visual culture for decades. He co-founded Push Pin Studios and designed the iconic “I ❤️ NY” logo, along with unforgettable posters, album covers, and magazine graphics. Glaser’s philosophy combined bold experimentation with a respect for craft, typography, and cultural storytelling.

His design ethos celebrates clarity, surprise, and joy. Milton believed that graphic design should be approachable, emotional, and visually communicative — turning even simple forms into memorable experiences.

Iconic Works

Legacy

Glaser’s work continues to influence designers worldwide. His bold use of color, inventive typography, and playful compositions paved the way for contemporary branding, poster design, and digital interfaces. Through his posters, logos, and editorial work, Milton demonstrated that graphic design can shape culture, evoke emotion, and endure across generations.

“There are three responses to a piece of design – yes, no, and WOW! Wow is the one to aim for.” — Milton Glaser
